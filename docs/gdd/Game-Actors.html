2.0 Attributes

Numerical values that represent an Actor's proficiency.

Icon Name Abbr. Description
Strength STR Ability to deal Physical Damage.
Defense DEF Ability to resist Physical Damage.
Accuracy ACC Ability to successfully execute a Physical attack
Evasion EVA Ability to successfully avoid a Physical attack
Intelligence ITL Ability to deal Magical Damage.
Wisdom WIS Ability to resist Magical Damage
Cognition COG Ability to successfully execute a Magical attack
Ego EGO Ability to successfully absorb a Magical attack

3.0 Affinities

Elemental Affinities are multipliers that represent an Actor's profficiency with the elements.

Icon Name Abbr. Description
Light LT Ability to deal and resist Light damage.
Air AR Ability to deal and resist Air damage.
Water WA Ability to deal and resist Water damange.
Wood WD Ability to deal and resist Wood damage.
Earth EA Ability to deal and resist Earth damage.
Fire FR Ability to deal and resist Fire damage.
Spark SK Ability to deal and resist Spark damage.
Dark DK Ability to deal and resist Dark damage.

The elements lie in opposition to each other.
Light opposes Dark.
Air opposes Spark.
Water opposes Fire.
Wood opposes Earth.

6.0 Origin

6.1 Zekarian

Use cold weapons: Swords, Spears, Bows, Axes, Staves and Flails. Can equip Sigils to use Action Skills. Can equip Mantras to use Support Skills.

6.2 Masari

Use hot weapons: Chainsaws, Drills, Muskets, Sprayers, Wrenches and Revolvers. Can equip Gadgets to use Action Skills. Can equip Implants to use Support Skills.

7.0 Classes

7.1 Archetypes

Greifer

Quick, close combat fighters that deal chip damage and ailments at a high rate.

Tank

Draws aggro and absorbs damage for the party.

Sniper

Deals heavy damage and ailments from afar at a slow rate.

Splasher

Deals AOE/DOT damage at close-medium range.

Healer

Heals HP/MP, revives and cures ailments.

Nuker

Deals heavy elemental damage from afar at a slow rate.
