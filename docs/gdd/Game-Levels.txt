Acropolis Trade and Development Center is built on an artificial island over 
Lake Leto as an extension to the main city of Acropolis serving as exhbition
space for corporations to have events such as the annual Masari Economic Summit.
The island is connected to the mainland by a single bridge and has piers to
allow for boats to land. The development center is divided into two wings (East
and West) consisting of exhbition halls and conference rooms on five levels. The
center's electrical facilities are automated using a central computer located in
the basement.
