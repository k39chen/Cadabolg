Story

Backstory

The game is set in the backdrop of the Endless War between the Holy Zekarian Empire and the Masari Union upon the continent of Antara. The conflict began over the control of border states that are rich with resources needed to sustain the growth of their respective nations, but eventually grew into a conflict of ideologies (see "Holy Zekarian Empire" and "Masari Union" for details).

The events of the game take place over a decade in the city-state of Adonis. Adonis is located in between two warring nations: Holy Zekarian Empire (to the East) and the Masari Union (to the West). Being located in a shielded valley in the Blackiron mountain range and being politically neutral, Adonis has managed to avoid the ravages of war ongoing in other parts of the continent.

Despite its relative isolation, Adonis has grown over the centuries to become a gathering place for Zekarian pilgrims and Masari entrepreneurs alike turning it into a major trading and academic hub on the continent. Being a melting pot of different cultures and practices, social matters often become heated and so order is brought about by Adonis's Militia.

The Militia acts as both its army and police force. It consists of citizens who volunteer for Militia service. They are trained in combat, war tactics and criminal investigation to enforce Adonis's laws. Militia members are usually organized into squads of 5-8. Roles vary from squad to squad, but there is typically a leader, one that organizes the squad's affairs, and a counselor, an experienced Militia member that acts in a advisory role.

Act 1

MC registers to join the Adonis Militia. MC joins Squad 2 are assigned to a mission inbvolving the crackdown of an arms smuggling ring that has operatives all over the city-state. Squad 2 catches the smugglers and discover that they are part of a conspiracy to nullify Adonis's neutrality with the warring nations.

The next day, the Adonis Assembly passes a bill to have the Militia arrest Zekarian and Masari diplomats and execute them at the city Forum. The Militia rejects these orders, but conspirators in their ranks start executing Zekarian and Masari diplomats on their own accord.

The news of the massacre reaches both nations. Within a few days, Adonis finds itself simultaneously beseiged by both Zekarian and Masari armies.

Design Note:

The player is allowed to fully explore Adonis although not all functions will be revealed. This is to contrast with Act 2 where Adonis will be locked off and so unlocking sectors of Adonis will be part of the player's incentive. This section is meant to be a tutorial for the main game, so it will be fairly directed and linear.

Act 2

The Adonis Assembly surrenders within a few hours of the siege. Both the Assembly and the Militia are dissolved as a joint military occupation of the city-state takes place. A temporary ceasefire between the armies is declared within the confines of Adonis resulting in the division of the city into Eastern and Western Blocs.

The Eastern Bloc consisting of the Villa, Temple, Garden and sectors of the city comes under Zekarian control. Zekarian laws are placed upon the former citizens of Adonis living there enforced by Zekarian Zealots.

The Western Bloc consisting of the Factory, University, Market and Slums sectors of the city comes under Masari controls. Likewise, Masari laws are enforced in these areas by Masari Marines. Between the two blocs is a Neutral Zone where negotiations are held. At the opposite edges of the Neutral Zone are barricades and watchtowers set up by both armies.

MC is broken out of a prison in the Neutral Zone by Militia survivors lead by Squad 2 and taken underground to join the Resistance. The Resistance plans to destabilize the foreign powers that occupy Adonis hoping to eventually gain enough support to oust them from the city altogether.

Squad 2 believes that both armies are looking for something called The Crucible within the confines of the city believing that it could turn the tides of the Endless War. MC is chosen to become the leader of the Resistance.

Design Note:

This is the meat of the game. The player begins with very little and over the course of this act accquires more Resistance fighters, more contacts, more missions and more rewards. There are certain story objectives that need to be completed, but it is entirely up to the player how they want to approach them. ALL OF THIS IS A BUILD UP TO ACT 3.

Act 3

Both sides have weakened due to the Resistance's efforts, however, all three sides have come to learn of the location of The Crucible which is located deep within the city. This begins a massive three-way battle between the Zekarian, Masari and Resistance fighters with the winner obtaining The Crucible, an item that can change the tides of the war.

Design Note:

This is the climax of the game's story. It is meant to be a parallel to the impossible battle at the end of Act 1, however, this time the player actually stands a chance.

Act 4

The Resistance obtains The Crucible and ousts its oppressors from Adonis. However, their stay left its mark. A previously unified city is now divided on a social level. With the dissolution of the Adonis Assembly and the control wrenched from Zekarian and Masari hands the question is: Who is in charge now?

The Resistance fighters have split into factions following individual members of former Squad 2 each with their own beliefs on how Adonis should be restructured. It is up to MC and power of The Crucible to choose how Adonis should be reformed and bring in the support to make that happen.

Design Note:

Act 4 has very little combat and there is more of an emphasis on the conversation/building aspects introduced earlier in the game. This is a resolution, which means that the story isn't over, but it is necessary to pave where the story is going.

Act 5

Depending on the MC's choice, Adonis is reborn into a new city. The survivors of the Zekarian and Masari occupation are now in the phase of finding closure with what happened during the occupation. Mobs of survivors are looking for someone to blame, which just happens to be you, the leader of the Resistance.

MC is taken to trial for war crimes and must defend him/her self for his/her actions. All the people that MC's helped or built trust with come as witnesses to support him/her. All the people that MC's screwed over will come to testify against you.

The day before the ending of the trial. MC will meet the survivors of Squad 2 at the burnt remains of the Militia Barracks where they will reflect on the events in the past few years. Some will be bitter while others sweet. Nonetheless, all agree that no matter what the verdict, MC will live on as the founder of a new Adonis.

The verdict is passed. Depending on how well MC defend his/her actions, the MC will be declared innocent or guilty. However, the verdict doesn't matter as all the pieces of the puzzle have come together.

Now begins the post-game.

Design Note:

Act 5 is conclusion of the story. It is to reflect upon the exact actions on the player and ask them if circumstances had been different would they have done it differently. It is also to show what the player has achieved by playing the game and wrap up loose ends or open new windows for future games.

Appendix

This section contains information to support the reader's understanding about the game's world.

Antara Continent

The continent

Holy Zekarian Empire

The Holy Zekarian Empire is an autocratic theocracy. The Immortal Emperor Zekar, a living connection to six Elemental Gods known collectively as The Pantheon, has complete authority over all those who worship him and the Elemental Gods. Zekar's words are few, but they are absolute and disobeying them is punishable by things worse than death.

History

In his earlier years (nine hundred years ago), Zekar preached about living in harmony with nature to deepen one's understanding about the world. Zekar taught his followers how to use the force of nature to empower oneself and defeat obstacles in what is now known as the venerable art of Magic. Zekar's followers would tattoo themselves with special symbols from the juices of fruit to improve their connection with nature and make their magic stronger. This is a practice that has remained in Zekarian culture over the years marking the coming of age of both men and women. Zekar's messianic status was further strengthened by his ability to tame ferocious monsters that threatened the lives of village communities. This gave rise to the Zekarian practice of domesticating monsters to assist people with their daily work.

In his later years (six hundred years ago), Zekar and his followers established the Holy Zekarian Empire as a nation along the eastern seaboard of Antara. Fundamental to the empire's foundation was the Covenant of the Pantheon, an order consisting of Zekar's most devoted followers. The Covenant's purpose was to spread the word of Zekar throughout the continent bringing in as many communities and followers as they could.

As many nations disagreed with the radical luddism preached by the Covenant, it became necessary for the Empire to defend its people and beliefs. The empire conscripted its young men and women to become Zealots, holy warriors that would be allowed to commit sins on their soul for the sake of the Empire. The Empire's Zealots, through the Covenant's extensive knowledge in magic and vast resources, were turned into an unstoppable military force.

Over two hundred years ago, the Zealots met their match when they encountered Masari Marines during the Zekarian occupation of a neutral state. This led to what is now called the Endless War, a stalemate between the two nations over complete control of all resources on the continent.

In recent years (hundred years ago to present), there has been a dramatic shift in power from Emperor Zekar to the Covenant, as the Covenant has managed to take over every aspect of the empire. There are few pundits who suggest that Zekar is little more than a figurehead nowadays and that someday, the Covenant will do away with Zekar's rule altogether.

Masari Union

The Masari Union is a political and economic conglomeration of many smaller nations that share an interest technology and how it can improve humans.
