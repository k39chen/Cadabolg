1.0 Setting

This section covers the details of the universe the game is set in.

1.1 World

[Cadabolg] takes place in the world named Eusfaria that is spherical planet resembling Earth in geological structure. Other heavenly bodies orbit Eusfaria similar to the Ptolemaic system of astronomy. These heavenly bodies include the Sun, Moon and the stars which make up eight constellations (Lux, Aer, Ea, Ved, Ierde, Fyr, Elektr, Umbra). The Sun and Moon lie on opposite ends of the Heavenly Sphere which rotates around Eusfaria. One full rotation of the Heavenly Sphere counts as a day on Eusfaria. The eight constellations lie on the Celestial Sphere which rotate around Eusfaria (but at a proportionately slower rate than the Heavenly Sphere). One full rotation of the Celestial Sphere counts as a year on Eusfaria, making each year eight months long corresponding to each constellation.

1.2 Holy Zekarian Empire

One of the two major political superpowers on Euria. The Holy Zekarian Empire was formed through a religious union of the eight Elemental Gods under a single group known as the Zekarrian Pantheon.

This sections contains biographies of the characters that originate from the Zekarian Empire. They are divided into groups of their allegiance.

2.1.1 Imperial Council

Composed of the Sages of the eight gods that make up the Zekarian Pantheon. Tasked with making political decisions on behalf of the empire. Sages are not elected by the people of the Empire, but by the religious groups that they come from based on interpretation of portents and omens that the Oracles recieve.
