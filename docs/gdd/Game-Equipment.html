3.0 Equipment

Equipment is the set of items that Actors can wear to enhance their performance in battle. Every Actor has 6 Equipment Slots where the Player can place Equipment corresponding to the 6 categories covered in this section. Note: Depending on the Race, Origin and Class of the Actor certain equipment restrictions may be present.

3.1 Weapon

Weapons are the implements that Actors use to attack each other. The player can use the Leader's weapon by pressing the "Confirm" button. There are many types of weapons that can only be equipped by certain Classes. Every type has a sub-type that exhbit different properties allowing the player to have some degree of customization over their Actors. Here are the different kinds of weapons:

3.2 Armor

Actors wear Armor to provide defense against enemy attacks and improve their capabilities in other areas.

3.2.1 Shield

Shields are used by Actors to block incoming attacks/projectiles. If these blocks are timed correctly, the damage from the attack is completely nullified.
